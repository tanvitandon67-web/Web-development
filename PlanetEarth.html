Our planet is full of incredible records,species,and facts

Steps to protect our Planet:

  1. Switch off lights when you leave a room
  2. Use a reuseable water bottle every day
  3. Walk or use a cycle instead of travelling by car
  4. Plant a tree in your neighbourhood
  5. Spread awarness-talk to your friends!!
