Fact 1: The Amazing Octopus

Octopuses are standouts among cephalopods, and among all invertebrates, for their large brains.

How smart are Octopuses

They can navigate mazes, solve problems, remember, predict, use tools and take apart just about anything from a crab to a lock — all but that last one sophisticated hunting behaviors.

Fact 1:Lightning strike

A lightning bolt can reach 54,000 degrees Fahrenheit, about five times hotter than the surface of our sun.

How many per year

Lightning strikes the United States 20 million times per year .
